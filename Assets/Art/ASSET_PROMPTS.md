# Pasta Defence — AI Art Prompts (dzine)

## Style Guide
> **Style suffix (append to ALL prompts):** "2D character sheet, cartoon anime style, thick black outlines, vibrant colors, clean flat shading, white background, no text"

## Recommended dzine Settings
- **Style:** 2D Character Sheet + Cartoon Anime
- **Style Intensity:** 0.5
- **Model:** Midjourney or GPT Image 1.5 (best for stylized cartoon characters)
- **Avoid:** Realistic, FLUX, 3D Render models
- **Output size:** 1024x1024 (square)
- **IMPORTANT:** All towers and enemies should have **faces, eyes, small arms/legs, and expressive personality** — think Plants vs Zombies meets Kitchen Chaos
